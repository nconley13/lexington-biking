Percent of Lexington, Kentucky Workforce Cycling to Work by Census Tract

Created for MAP 671

This map shows the percent of workers (16 and older) commuting to work by bike. Commuting characteristics data were retrieved from the American Community Survey. Data can be accessed at (Census Data). Lexington, Kentucky bike trails were retrived from Lexington Fayette Urban County Government Open Data Portal, can be accessed at ( Bike Trail Data). Bike shop locations were identified via QuickOSM.

Tools used in this project

  • QGIS 3.12
  • QuickOSM
